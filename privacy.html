Privacy Policy for Bundle

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

At Bundle, we believe that your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and explains how we handle information within the Bundle browser extension.

1. Data Collection & Transmission

The Bundle extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations, including area captures, full-page stitching, and color picking, are performed locally on your device.

  • No Cloud Storage: Images captured are processed in your browser's temporary memory and downloaded directly to your local machine.
  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels to monitor your behavior.
  • No External Servers: Bundle does not communicate with any external APIs or servers.

2. Permissions Use

Bundle requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • activeTab: Used only to capture the visual contents of the tab you are currently viewing.
  • downloads: Used to save your captures directly to your "Downloads" folder.
  • offscreen: Used to process high-resolution image stitching without slowing down your browser.

3. Third-Party Access

Since we do not collect any data, no data is ever sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official GitHub repository or the Chrome Web Store support dashboard.
